## Insurance & Payments

We accept most major insurance plans and are committed to making quality eye care accessible. Please contact our office to verify your specific coverage before your appointment.

### Accepted Insurance Types

- Medicare and Medi-Cal
- Blue Cross / Blue Shield
- Aetna
- Cigna
- United Healthcare
- And many more — please call to verify

### Payment Options

We accept all major credit cards, checks, and cash. We also work with patients on payment plans for elective procedures.

Please call our San Jose office at (408) 272-3706 with any billing or insurance questions.
