## LASIK Surgery

LASIK (Laser-Assisted In Situ Keratomileusis) is one of the most popular and effective vision correction procedures available. Our experienced surgeons offer both traditional LASIK and bladeless Intralase LASIK.

### Am I a Candidate?

Good LASIK candidates typically:
- Are at least 18 years old with stable vision
- Have adequate corneal thickness
- Are free of certain eye conditions such as keratoconus
- Have a prescription within treatable ranges

### Our LASIK Options

#### Intralase LASIK (All-Laser LASIK)
This bladeless technique uses a femtosecond laser to create the corneal flap, offering greater precision and predictability compared to traditional methods.

#### PRK (Photorefractive Keratectomy)
PRK is an excellent alternative for patients who are not ideal LASIK candidates. Instead of creating a flap, the outer layer of the cornea (epithelium) is gently removed before reshaping the cornea with the excimer laser.

### What to Expect

Most patients achieve 20/20 vision or better after LASIK. The procedure itself takes only a few minutes per eye, and many patients notice improved vision the very next day.
